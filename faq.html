What is Dining Points?

Standard Promotions: Promotions that provide immediate benefits to members. These promotions are based on a single transaction and don’t require member behavior tracking for promotion execution. For example, to clear out stock before winter, you offer 20% off jacket purchase on October 25. Cumulative Promotion: Promotions that reward members based on their longer-term behaviors, such as participation frequency or the amount of money spent to qualify for a reward. more information

How to Activate my Dining Reward?

All there is left is to make a reservation using the Book Now link in the email. For further instructions for how to book the reservation using your points please see the sections below for the different devices. Once the reservation is booked, you will receive a link to your Reward Card in your reservation confirmation email. Bring this Reward Card to your reservation and show it to the server in order to apply it toward your meal at the restaurant. You can print out your Reward Card or display it in the OpenTable app on your iPhone. more information

How can I find out how many Dining points I can collect?

Reservations eligible for Dining Points are displayed as part of the booking process. The number of points offered can be viewed at:

  1. Reservation details
  2. Confirmation pages
  3. Confirmation email

The number of Dining points you may collect varies according to:

  • Restaurant
  • Date Reservation
  • Time of the Reservation
  • Frequence of the Reservations
